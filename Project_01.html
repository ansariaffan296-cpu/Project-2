🌿 Introduction
Rose and Sunflower are popular flowering plants scientifically known as Rosa and Helianthus annuus. They belong to the families Rosaceae and Asteraceae respectively and are widely cultivated in tropical and temperate regions. They are among the most important ornamental and commercial plants in countries like India.
